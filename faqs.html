About

Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about publishing with Techspibus.

What is Techspibus Media and Publishing?

Techspibus is a full-spectrum publishing and media company registered in Lagos, Nigeria. We publish books, magazines, articles, and media rooted in ancient wisdom, philosophical depth, and scriptural insight. The name encodes our mission: Technology, Spirituality, and Business.

What kind of books does Techspibus publish?

We publish across four content pillars: Wisdom & Philosophy, Leadership & Enterprise, Culture & Society, and Personal Mastery. We accept non-fiction, literary fiction, poetry, and creative non-fiction that meets our editorial standards — work that is intellectually substantial, rooted in enduring sources, and produced with premium craft.

Do I need a literary agent to submit to Techspibus?

No. Techspibus accepts direct submissions from authors. Send your proposal to submissions@techspibus.com. We personally review all submissions — no agent or intermediary is required.

Do I need a completed manuscript to work with Techspibus?

We accept proposals at any stage. If your manuscript is complete, submit it for review. If you have an idea but need help completing it, our Writing Coach and Manuscript Development services can help you get there. If you are midway through your manuscript, start the conversation — we can advise on the best path forward.

How much does it cost to publish with Techspibus?

We customise our publishing packages to each book. Costs depend on factors including binding type, page count, format requirements, and the level of editorial and promotional support needed. Once we review your manuscript, we provide a detailed proposal outlining all fees, specifications, and timelines.

What does the publishing process look like?

Our five-step process covers Consultation (reviewing your manuscript and developing a proposal), Production (editing, design, typesetting, and cover creation), Promotion (a tailored marketing campaign for your book), Distribution (listing with global retailers), and Fulfilment (printing, shipping, and order management). Learn more about our process →

How will my book be distributed?

Techspibus distributes through Amazon KDP, IngramSpark, Draft2Digital, Apple Books, Kobo, Google Play, and local Nigerian retailers including Roving Heights and Okadabooks. Your book will be available in print, ebook, and audiobook formats globally.

Do I retain the rights to my work?

Yes. Techspibus authors retain copyright and ownership of their work. We publish under the Techspibus imprint with a publishing agreement, but the intellectual property belongs to you.

How will my manuscript be protected?

All submissions are treated as confidential. Techspibus will not use, reproduce, or distribute any material you submit without your written permission. We take the protection of your intellectual property seriously.

What kind of royalties can I expect?

Techspibus authors receive a percentage of the retail price on each book sold, as outlined in your publishing agreement. We are transparent about earnings — authors should publish to share their ideas with the world, with financial returns as a welcome but not guaranteed outcome.

How long does the publishing process take?

Timelines vary depending on the scope of editorial work, design complexity, and format requirements. A typical timeline from signed agreement to published book is 4 to 8 months. We provide a detailed timeline in your publishing proposal.

Does Techspibus offer promotional services?

Yes. Every Techspibus publication receives a tailored promotion campaign. We also offer additional promotional products and services — digital campaigns, media placement, launch events, audio and video production, and author platform development. Explore our services →
