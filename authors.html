For Authors

We Do Not Chase Trends.
We Publish Ideas That Create Them.

If your work draws from deep foundations and speaks to the questions that matter most, Techspibus may be your publisher.

Submissions

What We Are Looking For

Authors whose ideas are rooted in enduring wisdom — scripture, philosophy, history, lived experience tested against time — and who articulate those ideas with clarity, conviction, and craft.

Submission Criteria

Intellectual substance grounded in enduring sources

A distinctive voice that commands attention

Arguments that challenge assumptions, not recycle them

Craft that honours both content and reader

Wisdom that transcends sectarian boundaries

Ideas with global relevance and lasting impact

How to Submit

Send your proposal to submissions@techspibus.com with:

A one-page synopsis of your book's argument

A detailed table of contents

Two sample chapters (first and strongest)

Brief author biography and platform summary

Target audience and competitive titles

Word count (completed or projected)

We respond to all submissions within 30 days. Send a proposal first — unsolicited full manuscripts are not reviewed.
