Your Path

How to Become a Published Author in Five Steps

From manuscript to marketplace — a clear, structured path built around your work and your goals.

1

Consultation

Submit your completed manuscript to a dedicated publishing consultant who develops a customised proposal for your book.

2

Production

Your book is edited, a cover is designed, and the interior pages are typeset — every element crafted to Techspibus's premium standard.

3

Promotion

A tailored promotion campaign built for your specific book — social media, press outreach, and launch strategy designed to reach the right readers.

4

Distribution

Your book listed with leading retailers globally — Amazon, IngramSpark, and local Nigerian bookstores. Available in print, ebook, and audiobook.

5

Fulfilment

From order to doorstep — printing, shipping, and delivery managed so your readers receive their copy promptly and professionally.
