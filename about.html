About Techspibus

Technology. Spirituality. Business.

The disciplined, integrated architecture that equips leaders and institutions to navigate life and business.

The Origin

Why Techspibus Exists

Techspibus Media and Publishing was born from a conviction that the world's most enduring ideas — the ones that have guided civilisations, built empires, and transformed lives — deserve a publishing house that treats them with the gravity they demand.

The name carries intention. TECHSPIBUS = Technology · Spirituality · Business — the three domains that, when integrated with wisdom, produce work of lasting consequence.

Through teaching, writing, mentoring, and the building of creative solutions and systems, Techspibus shapes the thinking, behaviour, and development of Individuals, Businesses, Organisations, Nations, and Kingdoms — the IBONK continuum.

Why Techspibus

Why Publish With Us

Our philosophy is grounded in a conviction: the freedom to publish ideas of genuine substance should be available to every author whose work meets the standard. A book written for a hundred people is as worthy of publication as a book written for a million — if it says something that matters.

We work with you to develop a customised publishing package — including full-service production, global distribution, and tailored promotion — built specifically around your book, your audience, and your goals.

Our Principles

We enable authors to experience the transformative process of seeing their ideas published with the craft and authority they deserve.

We innovate the publishing process — integrating the precision of technology, the depth of spiritual wisdom, and the discipline of business into every stage.

We inspire and advise authors at every stage — from first idea to finished book — with the editorial rigour and strategic insight that defines our imprint.

We empower every author with the tools, distribution, and promotion needed to reach readers who will value their work.

Differentiation

How Techspibus Differs

From Commercial Publishers

You do not need an agent. Techspibus accepts direct submissions and personally reviews every manuscript — no intermediary standing between you and publication.

You retain creative control. Commercial publishers often own the rights and make all design and editorial decisions. At Techspibus, you approve every element — cover, layout, content — and retain your copyright.

You do not need a completed manuscript to start. Our Writing Coach and Manuscript Development services support authors at every stage of the writing process.

Your book reaches market faster. Commercial publishing timelines can stretch to 18 months or more. Techspibus works on timelines measured in months, not years.

From Other Publishing Service Companies

Intellectual depth. We are not a print shop. Every Techspibus publication is evaluated against our editorial standards — substance, authority, and craft. This is what separates a Techspibus book from a self-published book.

Personal approach. We take a personal approach to every book. Your work is not pushed through a standard template — it receives a customised publishing proposal built around its unique needs.

Full-service support. From consultation through production, promotion, distribution, and fulfilment — a dedicated team guides you through every stage.

Global reach, African roots. Rooted in Lagos with distribution infrastructure spanning Amazon, IngramSpark, and leading Nigerian retailers. Africa is our home, not our ceiling.
